AI-Powered Brand Voice Consistency Analysis

Ensure your content maintains consistent brand voice across your organization with AI-powered analysis that measures content alignment against your brand guidelines in real-time.

You need AI Brand Voice Analysis if

  • Multiple content creators work on your site and voice consistency varies
  • You need to maintain brand standards across different content types
  • Your organization requires content approval workflows with voice compliance
  • You want objective measurements of brand voice alignment

Key Features

  • Smart Brand Voice Analysis

    AI-powered analysis of content against your customizable brand voice guidelines with instant feedback.

  • Visual Performance Indicators

    Instant feedback through intuitive visual gauge displays showing brand alignment scores.

  • Seamless Integration

    Native integration with the Analyze framework for consistent reporting across all content analysis tools.

  • Flexible Customization

    Customizable brand voice guidelines through Drupal's hook system for organization-specific requirements.

Getting Started

  1. Configure AI provider settings at /admin/config/ai/providers
  2. Configure brand voice settings at /admin/config/analyze/brand-voice
  3. Enable/disable the analyzer per content type at /admin/config/content/analyze-settings
  4. Configure permissions at /admin/people/permissions#module-analyze_ai_brand_voice
  5. Access brand voice analysis through the Analyze tab on content

Additional requirements

This module requires:

  • Analyze module
  • AI module with a configured provider
  • Views Color Scales module (provides colored tables for report page)
