The AI Brand Voice Analysis is part of the AI module ecosystem. It provides AI-powered brand voice consistency analysis for Drupal content, measuring how well content aligns with your organization's brand voice guidelines. The brand voice guidelines can be customized through Drupal's hook system.

Features

  • AI-powered analysis of content against brand voice guidelines
  • Instant feedback through visual gauge display
  • Integration with the Analyze framework for consistent reporting
  • Customizable brand voice guidelines through hook_ai_brand_voice_alter()

Post-installation

  1. Configure AI provider settings at /admin/config/ai/providers
  2. Configure brand voice settings at /admin/config/analyze/brand-voice
  3. Enable/disable the analyzer per content type at /admin/config/content/analyze-settings
  4. Configure permissions at /admin/people/permissions#module-analyze_ai_brand_voice
  5. Access brand voice analysis through the Analyze tab on content

Additional requirements

This module requires:

  • Analyze module
  • AI module with a configured provider
  • Views Color Scales module (provides colored tables for report page)
